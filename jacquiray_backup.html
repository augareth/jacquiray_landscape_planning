Landscape Design by Jacqui Ray offers a comprehensive landscape design, planning and horticultural consultancy service. Our aim is to create a space you will love, and a garden you will enjoy, for many years.

With over 19 years experience in designing residential gardens, and drafting plans and documentation for local Council approval, Landscape Design by Jacqui Ray provides a complete design and planning service. Swimming Pool design, planning and approval is also a specialty.

From space constrained terraces to large rambling rural areas that can disillusion even an experienced gardener, we've helped clients achieve great results no matter the challenge. We look forward to discussing the individual challenges of your garden areas and the opportunity to transform the space into a thriving addition to your property and a place for friends and family to delight in.

About Us

Landscape Design by Jacqui Ray (LDBJR) offers a complete and experienced approach to Landscape Design. With over 19 years experience in designing residential gardens, and drafting plans and documentation for local Council approval, LDBJR provides a complete design and planning service.

Landscape Design by Jacqui Ray specialises in individually designed, site specific landscape solutions from small courtyards through to large residential sites. These can range in style from sleek outdoor spaces to water efficient landscapes, and natural, native style gardens.

Combined with Jacqui's background in horticulture and her passion for plants, you can be confident that the design will be functional, practical and will last for many years.

Jacqui's background and experience has extended beyond residential gardens into researching and designing for multiple TV lifestyle programs, including Domestic Blitz, and Better Homes and Gardens.
